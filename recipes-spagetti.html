Classic Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer 10 minutes. Season to taste.
  4. Toss spaghetti with sauce and serve immediately.
